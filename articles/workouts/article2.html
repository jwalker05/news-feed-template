Leg workouts to get you ready for the summer !

Da'Jah Hemsley 3:55 PM Workouts

My article will be showing leg workouts to get you toned for the Summer ! In this article you will see how to get healthy and toned legs step-by-step. These workouts will be fast and easy. You will see images to also support you in doing these step-by-step workouts. In this article there will also be workouts to get a bigger and firmer butt. So you can become bathing suit ready.If you would like to earn toned legs for the Summer, continue reading below !

Leg Lifts

  • First lay flat on your back
  • Next, put both of your arms behind your head
  • Then, stretch both legs out straight
  • Next, keep your legs together and lift them in the air vertical to your body

Mountain Climbers

  • First, stand up straight
  • Then, bend your body in a forward position so that your arms touch the floor
  • Next, began to move your legs in a forward and backward motion
  • Lastly, continue step three as though you were running in place.

Chair Squats

  • First, sit in the chair
  • Then, slide your body off the chair
  • Next, place your arms in a bending postion towards the end of the sit
  • Finally, use both your legs and your arms to push yourself up
