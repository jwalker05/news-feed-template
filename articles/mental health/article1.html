Motivation

Wpfreeware 6:49 AM Technology

Are you starting to workout again or trying to workout for the first time? Do you know where to start? Before you start considering having to work out in the gym or at home, start with what you are wanting to build, lose or tone with your body. Understand what you are looking for in yourself, find what tears you down and gives you stress to make that into motivation when you are going to the gym or working out at home. Today’s topic is Motivation, it is what builds you up to continue your journey of exercising, giving you strength to never give up when it is hard to complete pushing you to achieve your goals.

Being motivational is very important when it comes to exercising because it is a drives to continue to achieve your goal, it leads to go to the extreme and overcome any obstacles he/she had with themselves. Having motivation gets you ready for any problem you might have throughout your life, it makes you not want to quit what you have started. Motivation helps the body and mind, when you are working out you are being motivated to have the body you dreamed of having or wanting to have, giving you the strength to NEVER stop and push forward. It has been shown that motivation can help with anxiety and depression, according to “Depression and Anxiety: Exercise eases symptoms” (https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/depression/in-depth/depression-and-exercise/art-20046495) it says, “Research on depression, anxiety and exercise shows that the psychological and physical benefits of exercise can also help improve mood and reduce anxiety.”
